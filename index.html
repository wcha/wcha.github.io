Welcome to the WCHA!

We are the WCHA, or Western Canada Hyperloop Alliance, a partnership between multiple Western Canadian institutions!
Learn More

Who We Are

The West Coast Hyperloop Alliance is a cross-discipline community of students from the University of Victoria, University of British Columbia, University of Alberta and Simon Fraser University. As a team, we are committed to bring about the reality of a Hyperloop system.
