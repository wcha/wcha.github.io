Welcome to WCHA!

We are the WCHA, or Western Canada Hyperloop Alliance, an organization that is an alliance and partnership between multiple Western Canadian institutions!

Headquatered at the University of Victoria, the WCHA is formed also by the University of British Columbia, Simon Fraser University, the British Columbia Institute of Technology, and the University of Alberta. Together, our goal is to use the resources, research, and knowledge of our various institutions to help contribute to the improvement in public transportation infrastructure.
